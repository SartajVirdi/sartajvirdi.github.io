04

ML Drift Monitor — Observability for production ML

An ML monitoring system that detects feature drift (using KS and PSI tests) and performance decay across sklearn, XGBoost, LightGBM, and CatBoost models. The goal is to catch silent model decay before it hurts production pipelines.

Retraining kicks in automatically when F1 drops or drift crosses a threshold. Includes JSON/CSV audit logs and a Streamlit dashboard for easy monitoring.
